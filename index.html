Class Description
Title Description
Introduction to BYU's Record Linking Lab Come and see how the Record Linking Lab is developing and using powerful technology to increase the quality and coverage of the Family Tree on FamilySearch. You will see a demonstration of volunteer resources that are easy to use, such as automated indexing and customized record hints, that will help you and other people build the Family Tree, as well as find relatives who need temple ordinances performed.
How to Find and Enhance Photos of Ancestors This class will help find possible photos of ancestors who you have no photos of... at minimum a headstone photo or other photos you are not aware of that might exist on Find a Grave.  Also, how to find possible school yearbook photos using Ancestry.com.  Then take these photos or any photo that you would like to enhance by making the photo clearer, repair scratches, change photos to color using the tools from MyHeritage.com.
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
   
Swedish Ancestry An overview of Swedish research. The records currently available on family search and my heritage. As well as what's available at the family history center. Also the Swedish Genealogical Word List in the family search wiki.
